About Me

I’m Brendan James Duff. I mentor men who want to steady themselves and move with clarity. My approach is simple and direct: listen closely, cut the noise, and build what lasts — presence, communication, and daily action.

I call this The Noble Masculine — presence without posturing, responsibility without ego, and strength that steadies others. We work practically: how you speak, how you show up, and how you hold yourself when life gets loud.

Pause Prevention Project

Alongside Forge Mentoring, I created the Pause Prevention Project — the home for my suicide prevention work. It includes Pause for Suicide, a simple human invitation to stop, breathe, and find steady ground, and a one-day intervention built on See • Say • Listen • Link.

If you’re exploring prevention for yourself, a friend, or a team, this is where I bring that work together.

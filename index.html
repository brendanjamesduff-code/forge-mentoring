About Me

I mentor men who want to steady themselves and move with clarity. My approach is simple and direct: listen closely, cut the noise, and build what lasts — presence, communication, and daily action.

I call this The Noble Masculine: presence without posturing, responsibility without ego, and strength that steadies others. Together we work practically on how you speak, how you carry yourself, and how you choose steady action when life gets loud.

Pause Prevention Project

Alongside Forge Mentoring, I created the Pause Prevention Project — the home for my suicide prevention work. It includes Pause for Suicide, a simple human invitation to stop, breathe, and find steady ground, and a one-day intervention built on See • Say • Listen • Link.

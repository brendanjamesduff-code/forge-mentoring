About Me

I’m Brendan Duff, founder of Forge Mentoring. My work is about balance, presence, and navigating life with strength and compassion. I bring my lived experience from the military, years of service, and a personal philosophy rooted in the Noble Masculine. I’ve spent my life helping others through crisis, loss, and transformation, and now I offer that support through mentoring and guidance.
